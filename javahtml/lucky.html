ANNOUNCEMENT: Tech geeks are announcing NIRVANA 2.0!

know more

TECH GEEKS are overjoyed to share with you the details of the all new event NIRVANA 2.0.

Tech Geeks Club, in collaboration with the School of Computing, is organizing Nirvana Tech Fest 2025 on October 17 and 18. This two-day event will bring together students from various schools and colleges to celebrate technology, creativity, and innovation. The fest will feature a wide range of activities including hackathons, workshops, seminars, science exhibitions, coding competitions, and certification examinations. In addition to the technical events, departmental stalls will showcase innovative projects and ideas, while the evenings will be highlighted with a vibrant DJ night to foster networking and celebration. Your task is to design and develop a dedicated website exclusively for Nirvana Tech Fest 2025 that effectively communicates the schedule, event details, and registration information. The website should be visually appealing, easy to navigate, and responsive across devices, serving as the digital face of the Fest and capturing the energy and spirit of this grand celebration of technology
